Collected Variables

The full list of non-imaging variables included in the EuCanImage collections are described in the catalogue, together with its availability in each dataset and some aggregated statistics displaying ranges, distributions, etc.

This is a summary table of the clinical and epidemiological variables collected.

Variable Description Type
Sex Biological sex of the patient (male/female). Clinical
Age at diagnosis Patient's age when the cancer was diagnosed. Epidemiological
Radiotherapy Whether the patient received radiation therapy as part of treatment. Clinical
Surgery Whether surgical treatment was performed. Clinical
