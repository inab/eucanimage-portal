AI modeling

EuCanImage is creating a collection of radiomics methods and AI algorithms for building novel integrative AI models from large-scale imaging and non-imaging data. They are offered through the AI Virtual Research Environment, a portable computational environment for supporting the development and validation of AI tools.

  • Tools for cancer image feature extraction and selection.
  • Machine-learnig pipeline for integrated predictive modelling.
