A trusted data management and curation platform for enhancing AI cancer imaging

Leveraging the most established, secure and tested data infrastructures and curation tools

EuCanImage consortium has built a secure, federated and large-scale European cancer platform, with capabilities to greatly enhance the potential of artificial intelligence (AI) in oncology. As a result, completely new data resources over 250,000 single subjects are being curated, annotated and made public at the platform to investigate unmet clinical needs. The platform includes:
Novel curated and annotated imaging data cross-linked to biological and health records.
